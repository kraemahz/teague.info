\section{Notation Summary}
\label{app:notation}

Table~\ref{tab:notation7} collects the principal symbols specific
to this paper.  Symbols inherited from earlier
papers~\citep{lasser2026poset, lasser2026horizon, lasser2026scm,
lasser2026exo, lasser2026phase}, including $\volL$
(capability volume), $G_t$ (global capability graph), $P$, $P_t$
(capability poset), $\Pathway$ (exercise pathway), $\Risk$,
$\Risk_j$ (concentration risk), $a_j$ (agent index), and
$T^{\mathrm{risk}}_j$ (risk-trust factor), retain their original
meanings and are not redefined here.
